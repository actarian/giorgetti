Giorgetti's places to live in Spain

The three villas designed by Femont Galvan Designers are in the most wonderful locations in Spain and they will make you dream!

Giorgetti pieces design the spaces of these projects in a wonderful sequence of environments and moods.

Proportions, materials, light and colour are signature elements of Luis Barragan’s architecture in "La Reserva de Sotogrande". This project combines a seamless design with a timeless flair. The building gracefully emerges from a wide golf course to enjoy the outdoor environment. From the building to every detail, this villa has been entirely customised and is characterised by wide spaces, bright rooms and precious materials.

A fresh, elegant and sophisticated aesthetic interior shared by the "Mews II" project in the British Gibraltar. Influenced by the detailing of historic Arab ornaments, the studio developed its own contemporary Moroccan decoration in a minimal space. The combination between light colours and bold materials defines the space aesthetically and functionally.

A refined and warm atmosphere like that of the "Valderrama" project: a selection of natural materials, wood, leather, textiles and marble, in combination with a subtle sandy palette. The balance between materials and colours is the key to achieve this home feeling of effortless cosiness.

Godron

Giorgetti Atmosphere Collection

Realized in collaboration with Listone Giordano, Godron is an architectural surface made up of triangle and rhombus-shaped tiles that enables to obtain multiple patterns thanks to two directions of laying it. The decoration is obtained from the characteristic geometry of the tiles and from 3 different gloss levels and colour saturation of walnut canaletto wood. Godron can be used both as parquet and boiserie.
Space Black

Moodboard Giorgetti

Characterized by white, gray and black shades, it gives volume to the different patterns of the fabrics.

They recall the contrast between the black outer space and the brightness of stars and planets. Black is synonym of elegance both in fashion and in interior design and the veining of Portoro marble enhance it.

DANIEL STEEGMANN MANGRANÉ
Until February 19th, 2020
pirellihangarbicocca.org

Daniel Steegmann Mangrané’s first exhibition in Italy ponders on the relationship between culture and nature.

The artist’s interest in biology has led him to analyse complex ecological systems and introduce the natural world into his works.

In Steegmann Mangrané’s practice numerous are the references to the rain forest in Brazil—such as branches, leaves, and insects—which interwoven with geometric forms and abstract motifs engender a reflection on the complex dynamics between the elements that surround us.
FRAGILITY AND BEAUTY
Until October 31st, 2019
museoscienza.org

The exhibit promoted by the European Space Agency (ESA) in collaboration with the Italian Space Agency (ASI) returns to the Science Museum for a new installation with the most recent Earth images taken by satellites.

The exhibition, curated by Viviana Panaccia, aims to raise public awareness on climate change and sustainable development, their impact on terrestrial ecosystems and the consequences on the planet's future.
